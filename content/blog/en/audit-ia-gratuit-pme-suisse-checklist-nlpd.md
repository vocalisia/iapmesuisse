## Why a free AI audit (not just a demo)

A demo shows you a tool. An AI audit examines **your** company: workflow, volumes, compliance, team, data. The difference is critical in 2026: with the **AI Act** applying extraterritorially to Swiss SMEs serving EU clients, and the **nFADP** in force since September 2023, picking an AI tool without an audit is like signing a contract without reading it.

Concretely, a serious free AI audit delivers:
- **Process mapping**: 5 to 10 processes measured in hours/week

## The 5 most common quick wins in Swiss SMEs

### 1. Drafting reports, quotes, summaries
Typical saving: **60 to 70%** of writing time. Tools: ChatGPT Team, Microsoft Copilot, Claude Pro. Cost: CHF 18 to 30/user/month.

### 2. Inbound email pre-processing
Sorting, classification, reply suggestions. Saving: **40 to 50%** of inbox time. nFADP-compliant with Microsoft 365 hosted in Switzerland or EU.

### 3. B2B prospecting automation
Lead enrichment, scoring, personalised sequences. Caution: the **AI Act** classifies profiled automated prospecting as limited risk (transparency required).

### 4. Multilingual 24/7 customer service
Specialised FAQ chatbots + human handover. Saving: 30 to 50% of level-1 tickets resolved automatically.

### 5. Decision support in accounting
Automatic transaction categorisation, anomaly detection, pre-input. See concrete cases in our [Swiss SME case studies](/en/blog/etudes-cas-pme-romandes-ia).

## Common mistakes the audit reveals

- **Generalised AI shadow IT**: 60 to 80% of staff use free ChatGPT with client data (Art. 8 nFADP breach)
- **Uncontracted processors**: no data processing agreement with OpenAI, Microsoft, Anthropic
- **Quick wins already within reach**: 1 to 2 automations at -50% time achievable in 2 weeks, ignored
- **Tool over-investment**: 5 to 8 stacked AI subscriptions when 2 would suffice
- **No training**: 15-20% adoption instead of 70% potential (see [AI training for executives](/en/blog/formation-ia-dirigeants))

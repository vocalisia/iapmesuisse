## 5. Swiss FADP compliance

WhatsApp Business automation involves processing personal data — names, phone numbers, conversation content. Compliance with the nFADP is non-negotiable:

- **Clear disclosure at first contact**: every new WhatsApp conversation must include an explicit mention that the user is interacting with an AI system, along with a link to your privacy policy.
- **"Speak to a human" button**: always accessible within the conversation, with a guaranteed response time commitment.
- **Storage of conversations**: conversations must be stored in the EU or Switzerland. Use n8n self-hosted at Infomaniak (Switzerland) or Make with EU data residency for your orchestration layer.
- **Data minimisation**: the AI should only collect and store the data strictly necessary for the service. Conversation logs should be automatically purged after a defined retention period (30 to 90 days is standard).
- **Right to deletion**: your process must allow a contact to request deletion of their conversation history, as guaranteed under nFADP.

## FAQ: WhatsApp Business AI for Swiss SMEs

**Q: Can a small Swiss SME with no technical team deploy WhatsApp Business automation?**
Yes. The BSP handles account activation and API access. No-code orchestration platforms like Make or n8n (with its visual interface) allow operations managers to build conversation flows without coding. Several Swiss digital agencies specialise in turnkey WhatsApp automation deployments for SMEs, delivering a production-ready system within 4 to 6 weeks.

**Q: What languages does the AI support natively for WhatsApp conversations?**
Modern LLMs support French, German, Italian, and English at a native level — including Swiss-specific vocabulary and formulations. The AI auto-detects the language of each incoming message and responds in the same language throughout the conversation. A single workflow serves the entire Swiss linguistic landscape without separate configurations per language.

**Q: Is WhatsApp Business automation suitable for regulated industries such as healthcare or finance?**
Yes, with appropriate configuration. Healthcare and financial SMEs must ensure all personal data is handled on FADP-compliant infrastructure (Swiss or EU hosting), conversations are encrypted at rest, and the AI does not provide advice that crosses into regulated territory (medical diagnosis, investment recommendations). The AI handles scheduling, administrative tasks, and general FAQs; regulated advice is always escalated to a qualified human. Several Swiss medical practices and fiduciaries already operate compliant WhatsApp automation in production.

## Sectors in Valais Where AI Makes the Biggest Difference

### Tourism and Hospitality

Valais is Switzerland's leading tourist canton. The hospitality sector employs over 15,000 people and generates annual revenues exceeding one billion francs. AI is being deployed in several key areas:

**Reservation management and customer relations.** Chatbots and voice agents handle inquiries in French, German, English, and increasingly Chinese — the four most common languages of Valais customers. A voice agent can manage availability requests, confirmations, and modifications 24/7, reducing reception workloads by 40 to 60%.

**Revenue management.** AI analyzes demand, competitor occupancy rates, local events, and weather conditions to optimize prices in real time. Hotels using these tools report an increase in RevPAR (revenue per available room) of 8 to 15%.

**Personalized stays.** Analysis of past preferences, tailored activity recommendations, and personalized pre-stay messages: AI enables a premium experience without a dedicated CRM team.

### Viticulture and Agri-Food Industry

Viticulture is the economic and cultural identity of Valais. Wineries and agri-food companies find concrete applications for AI:

- **Harvest forecasting** using weather and historical data analysis
- **Supply chain optimization** (orders, inventory, deliveries)
- **Automated digital marketing**: newsletters, social media, e-commerce
- **Traceability and certification**: automation of regulatory documents

### Professional Services (Fiduciaries, Lawyers, Consultants)

The professional services sector represents a significant portion of employment in Sion. Fiduciary offices, law firms, and consulting companies particularly benefit from AI for:

- **Automatic document review** (contracts, balance sheets, tax declarations)
- **Standardized document generation** (template contracts, letters, reports)
- **Automated regulatory monitoring** (tax changes, legislative updates)
- **Internal assistants** answering employee questions on legal bases

A fiduciary office in Sion that deployed an internal AI assistant reports a gain of 2 to 3 hours per employee per week on regulatory information searches.

### Retail

Valais retailers face increased competition from e-commerce. AI provides tools to compete:

- **Sales forecasting** and inventory optimization
- **Personalized promotions** based on purchase history
- **WhatsApp chatbots** for orders and product inquiries
- **Customer review analysis** to identify areas for improvement

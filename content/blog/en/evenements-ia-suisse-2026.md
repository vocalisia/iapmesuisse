# AI Events in Switzerland: 2026 Agenda

Switzerland's artificial intelligence scene is one of the most dynamic in Europe. With institutions like EPFL, ETH Zürich, the UN campus in Geneva, and a thriving start-up ecosystem, there are countless opportunities to learn, network, and discover the latest AI innovations.

This article lists the major events of 2026, providing practical information to help SME leaders choose the most relevant gatherings for their business.

## Why Attend AI Events

For an SME leader, attending AI events offers several tangible benefits:

- **Technological monitoring**: Understand trends and anticipate developments
- **Networking**: Meet peers, providers, and experts
- **Continuous training**: Deepen specific topics in a condensed format
- **Inspiration**: Discover use cases applicable to your company
- **Recruitment**: Identify talent within the Swiss AI ecosystem

## Major Events — First Half of 2026

### AI+X Summit — Zürich

- **Date**: April 15-16, 2026
- **Location**: Hallenstadion, Zürich
- **Format**: Conference + exhibition (2 days)
- **Themes**: AI applied to industry, healthcare, finance, and SMEs
- **Price**: 490 – 1,290 CHF depending on the pass
- **Target audience**: Decision-makers, entrepreneurs, CTOs
- **Why attend**: The largest AI event in German-speaking Switzerland, featuring a dedicated SME track since 2025. Excellent networking opportunities with the Swiss tech ecosystem.

### Swiss AI Conference — Lausanne

- **Date**: May 7-8, 2026
- **Location**: SwissTech Convention Center, EPFL
- **Format**: Academic and industrial conference (2 days)
- **Themes**: AI research, industrial applications, ethics, regulation
- **Price**: 350 – 850 CHF (discounted rate for SMEs)
- **Target audience**: Researchers, industry professionals, leaders
- **Why attend**: A meeting point between Swiss academia (EPFL, IDIAP) and industry. The practical workshops on day two are particularly useful for SMEs.

### Digital Day Switzerland — Multiple Cities

- **Date**: May 21, 2026
- **Location**: Geneva, Lausanne, Bern, Zürich, Lugano (simultaneously)
- **Format**: Day of workshops, conferences, and demonstrations
- **Themes**: Digital transformation, AI, cybersecurity, data
- **Price**: Free
- **Target audience**: General public and SMEs
- **Why attend**: Free and accessible, it's the perfect opportunity for a first contact with the Swiss AI ecosystem. Practical workshops are designed for non-technicians.

### Geneva AI Week — Geneva

- **Date**: June 9-13, 2026
- **Location**: CICG and Palais des Nations, Geneva
- **Format**: Full week (conferences, workshops, networking)
- **Themes**: AI governance, AI and sustainable development, humanitarian applications
- **Price**: 200 – 1,500 CHF depending on the events
- **Target audience**: International organizations, businesses, civil society
- **Why attend**: Geneva is a global hub for AI governance. This week offers a unique international perspective and high-level discussions on regulation and ethics.

### Forum Économique Romand — Lausanne

- **Date**: June 18, 2026
- **Location**: Beaulieu, Lausanne
- **Format**: Business forum (1 day)
- **Themes**: Economy in French-speaking Switzerland, innovation, AI and SMEs
- **Price**: 180 – 450 CHF
- **Target audience**: Leaders of SMEs in French-speaking Switzerland
- **Why attend**: A flagship event for the regional economy. The "AI and SMEs" panel features leaders who have successfully integrated AI into their businesses.

## Major Events — Second Half of 2026

### SWITCHdays — Bern

- **Date**: September 10-11, 2026
- **Location**: Kursaal, Bern
- **Format**: Technology conference (2 days)
- **Themes**: Digital infrastructure, cloud, AI, cybersecurity
- **Price**: 200 – 600 CHF
- **Target audience**: IT managers, technology decision-makers
- **Why attend**: A reference technical event, particularly relevant for infrastructure and security issues related to AI.

### Swiss Digital Economy Conference — Zürich

- **Date**: October 1, 2026
- **Location**: Kongresshaus, Zürich
- **Format**: Conference + networking (1 day)
- **Themes**: Digital economy, AI, fintech, e-commerce
- **Price**: 390 – 890 CHF
- **Target audience**: Entrepreneurs, investors, leaders
- **Why attend**: Focus on the tangible economic impact of digitalization. Keynotes highlight inspiring Swiss success stories.

### AI in Business Summit — Geneva

- **Date**: October 22-23, 2026
- **Location**: Palexpo, Geneva
- **Format**: Conference + exhibition (2 days)
- **Themes**: AI applied to businesses, automation, digital transformation
- **Price**: 450 – 1,200 CHF
- **Target audience**: Decision-makers, operational managers
- **Why attend**: 100% business-oriented, featuring concrete use cases and an exhibition space to test solutions. Dedicated SME track with practical workshops.

### Salon RH Suisse — Geneva

- **Date**: November 4-5, 2026
- **Location**: Palexpo, Geneva
- **Format**: Professional fair + conferences (2 days)
- **Themes**: HR, recruitment, training, AI and talent management
- **Price**: Free (registration required)
- **Target audience**: HR directors, HR managers, leaders
- **Why attend**: Innovations in AI for [recruitment](/fr/blog/ia-recrutement-suisse) and HR management are showcased. Free and accessible.

### Digitaltag — Multiple Cities

- **Date**: November 15, 2026
- **Location**: 15 Swiss cities simultaneously
- **Format**: Open house day and workshops
- **Themes**: Digitalization, AI, digital inclusion
- **Price**: Free
- **Target audience**: General public and SMEs
- **Why attend**: A national awareness event, ideal for engaging your teams in digital reflection.

## How to Maximize an AI Event

### Before the Event

1. **Define your objectives**: What are you looking for? Providers, ideas, contacts?
2. **Identify key speakers** and prepare specific questions
3. **Plan your schedule**: Select priority sessions and workshops
4. **Prepare your pitch**: Be ready to explain your company and AI needs in 30 seconds

### During the Event

1. **Attend practical workshops** rather than generic keynotes
2. **Ask specific questions**: "How much does it cost?", "What ROI for an SME of my size?"
3. **Exchange contact details** with 5 to 10 relevant individuals
4. **Take structured notes**: Ideas, contacts, actions to follow up on

### After the Event

1. **Send a message** to each contact within 48 hours
2. **Share learnings** with your team
3. **Identify 2-3 concrete actions** to implement within the month
4. **Plan** your next event

## Priority Events Not to Miss

If you can only attend three events in 2026, we recommend:

1. **Digital Day Switzerland** (May): Free, accessible, perfect for beginners
2. **AI in Business Summit** (October, Geneva): The most focused on practical applications for SMEs
3. **A local monthly meetup**: To build a regular network within the French-speaking AI ecosystem

For leaders looking to go further, an Innosuisse or Digitalswitzerland support program is a particularly relevant complementary investment.

The Swiss AI ecosystem is rich, dynamic, and accessible.

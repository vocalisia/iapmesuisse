## Compliance with the nFADP: An Imperative for Swiss SMEs

In Switzerland, the application of AI models must comply with the new Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP), which imposes strict requirements on the management and processing of personal data. SMEs must ensure that the AI solutions they adopt comply with these rules to protect their clients' privacy and avoid potential sanctions. Microsoft, aware of these challenges, emphasises the security and compliance of its technologies.

# Free AI tools for Swiss SMEs: top 15 (2026)

Getting started with AI in an SME should never begin with a large investment. In 2026, **more than 15 free or freemium tools** allow a Swiss SME — whether in the Romandy, Valais, German-speaking or Ticino region — to rapidly prototype AI automations, evaluate their ROI and build competence with no financial commitment.

> For the general context: [pillar guide on AI automation for Swiss SMEs](/fr/automatisation-ia-pme-suisse).

## 1. Conversational AI models

### ChatGPT Free (OpenAI)

- **Use cases**: drafting letters, summaries, first-pass translations, brainstorming.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: data goes to the USA. Avoid for sensitive business data.

### Claude Free (Anthropic)

- **Use cases**: long document analysis, code, regulatory reasoning.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: US data centre, but Anthropic does not use customer data for training. Better than OpenAI for semi-sensitive data.

### Mistral Le Chat

- **Use cases**: serious European alternative, natively multilingual in FR/DE/IT.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: EU data centre (France, Germany). **Recommended for sovereignty-aware Swiss SMEs.**

### Microsoft Copilot Free

- **Use cases**: integrated in Edge and Bing, ideal for web-augmented research.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: European data centre possible with a correctly configured Microsoft 365 EU tenant.

## 2. Research and knowledge organisation

### NotebookLM (Google)

- **Use cases**: analysis of multiple internal documents (PDF, Word), summaries and audio podcasts.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: US and EU data centres depending on Workspace configuration.

### Perplexity Free

- **Use cases**: web search with cited sources.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: US data centre. Use only for public data.

## 3. Automation and workflows (no-code)

### n8n self-hosted

- **Use cases**: orchestrate complex workflows between Bexio, Microsoft 365 and CRM.
- **Free**: yes, self-hosted with no limits.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: maximum when hosted at Infomaniak or Exoscale.
- **Recommended** for any SME with a long-term investment perspective. Full guide: [n8n self-hosted for Swiss SMEs](/fr/blog/n8n-auto-heberge-pme-suisse-nlpd).

### Make (formerly Integromat)

- **Use cases**: visual automation for beginner SMEs.
- **Free**: yes, up to 1,000 operations/month.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: EU data centre (Germany). Acceptable for most Swiss SMEs.

### Zapier Free

- **Use cases**: 5 simple workflows per month.
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: US data centre. Avoid for sensitive data.

## 4. Image generation and editing

### Microsoft Designer (Copilot Designer)

- **Use cases**: marketing visuals, social media posts, presentations.
- **Free**: yes, with daily quota.

### Canva with Magic Studio

- **Use cases**: quick design for non-graphic designers.
- **Free**: yes (basic), Magic Studio paid.

## 5. Audio and transcription

### Whisper (OpenAI) via local

- **Use cases**: multilingual transcription (FR/DE/IT/EN) — excellent quality.
- **Free**: yes, locally (downloadable open-source model).
- **Swiss FADP compliance**: maximum when run locally.

## 6. Choosing the right free tools for your Swiss SME

4 questions to guide your choice:

1. **Are the data processed sensitive?** Yes → avoid US tools, prefer Mistral or n8n self-hosted.
2. **How many internal users?** More than 5 → budget for a paid plan.
3. **Temporary (test) or permanent?** Permanent → prefer tools with transparent licences.
4. **What integrations does your stack need?** Bexio, Microsoft 365 → check Make or n8n connector availability.

## 7. Top 3 free combos for Swiss SMEs

### Combo "gentle start"
- Mistral Le Chat + Make Free + Microsoft Designer.
- **Cost: CHF 0.** Lets you test AI on 80% of common SME use cases.

### Combo "maximum sovereignty"
- n8n self-hosted (Infomaniak) + Mistral via API + Whisper local.
- **Cost: ~CHF 30–50/month** for the server.

### Combo "commercial productivity"
- Claude Free + Make Free + HubSpot CRM Free.
- **Cost: CHF 0.**

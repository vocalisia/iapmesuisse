# AI Training Prices for Swiss SMEs 2026: Canton Comparison in CHF

How much does AI training for an SME cost in Switzerland in 2026? The range goes from 0 to CHF 25,000 depending on format, duration, canton and personalisation level. This article spells out **real** prices per service category, mobilisable cantonal and federal funding, and quality criteria beyond the price tag. All in CHF, no VAT mark-ups (trainers under the Art. 293B small-business regime do not charge VAT), including obligations imposed by the **AI Act** and **nFADP**.

## The 5 AI training formats and their price ranges

| Format | Duration | Audience | Price CHF |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conference / keynote | 1 to 1.5 h | 10 to 200 people | 0 to 1,500 CHF |
| Discovery webinar | 1 to 2 h | 5 to 30 people | 0 to 297 CHF |
| Practical workshop | 0.5 to 1 day | 6 to 15 people | 800 to 2,500 CHF |
| Custom team training | 1 to 5 days | 4 to 12 people | 1,800 to 12,500 CHF |
| Coaching programme | 5 to 15 days spread | leadership + team | 5,000 to 25,000 CHF |

Upper ranges (Geneva, Zurich, finance/health) integrate sector expertise, custom material, post-training follow-up, and documented AI Act + nFADP compliance.

## Detailed comparison by format

### Conference / keynote
**0 to 500 CHF** for chamber of commerce or association talks, **800 to 1,500 CHF** for in-house corporate (90 min, up to 100 people). This format alone does not cover the Art. 4 AI Act training obligation.

### Discovery webinar (often free)
Classic lead-generation format. **Free** at serious providers, up to **297 CHF** for a structured premium webinar.

### Practical workshop 0.5 to 1 day
Most cost-effective entry point for an SME:
- **800 CHF** half-day (4h, up to 8 people) in Valais or Jura
- **1,200 CHF** half-day in Lausanne, Neuchâtel, Fribourg
- **1,800 to 2,500 CHF** full day (7h) in Geneva or Zurich

### Custom team training
Premium format for SMEs of 5-20 people:
- **1,800 CHF/day** for an independent trainer in French-speaking Switzerland
- **2,500 CHF/day** for an established firm with custom material
- **3,500 CHF/day** for Claude Code, dev, or advanced marketing training

A complete team programme (4-5 days spread over 3 months): **8,000 to 15,000 CHF** all-inclusive.

### Full coaching programme
For SMEs in deep digital transformation. Includes diagnosis, leadership training, team training, tool implementation, 6-12 month follow-up. Price: **15,000 to 25,000 CHF**. Cantonal + InnoSuisse funding mobilisable.

## Quality criteria beyond price

- **eduQua certification** or equivalent (compliant with Art. 4 AI Act)
- **Multilingual**: can the trainer switch FR/DE based on profiles?
- **Custom-fit**: sector adaptation (health, finance, legal, industry)
- **Explicit tools**: ChatGPT Team, Copilot, Claude, Mistral, Apertus
- **Documented compliance**: nFADP + AI Act baked into pedagogy
- **Post-training follow-up**: 30-60 days of support included
- **Measurable ROI**: pre/post productivity KPIs

## Common SME budget mistakes

- **Picking the cheapest "to test"**: 60% of trainings under CHF 500/day fail their pedagogical promises
- **Skipping leadership training**: 80% of AI projects fail when the C-suite isn't trained first (see [AI training for executives](/en/blog/formation-ia-dirigeants))
- **Training the whole company at once**: prefer 2-3 waves spread over 6-12 months
- **Ignoring cantonal funding**: 30 to 50% recoverable
- **Not requiring AI Act compliance**: training without Art. 4 is useless

## FAQ

### Does price depend on number of trainees?
Partly yes. Beyond 8-10 people, workshops lose interactivity. Better two 8-person sessions than one 16-person session.

### Can I fund AI training via Covid/FCE schemes?
Covid programmes are closed. But the **Cantonal Employment Fund (FCE)** or regional aid often remain mobilisable. Check with your cantonal employment office.

### How long is an AI training valid?
12 to 18 months max. AI evolves too fast. Plan an annual refresh (1/2 day at CHF 600-1,200) to stay compliant with Art. 4 AI Act.

### Video vs in-person: price difference?
Video: **-15 to -25%** on trainer day rate. But loss of group dynamics. Recommended: hybrid with 1 in-person day + 2 follow-up video sessions.

### Does management need different training?
Yes. Leadership training (1/2 day at CHF 1,200-2,500) covers strategy, governance, AI Act — not tools. Indispensable before training the team.
